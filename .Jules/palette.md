## 2026-01-04 - Side-by-Side Comparisons for Visual Tools
**Learning:** Users gain much more confidence in "interpolation" or "generation" tools when they see the Start, Middle, and End states side-by-side, rather than just the result.
**Action:** For any script generating intermediate frames or transformations, always plot the input, result, and target (if available) in a single subplot for instant visual verification.
